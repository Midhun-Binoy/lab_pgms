Welcome to MES T.O. Abdulla Memorial College, Kunnukara

MES T.O. Abdulla Memorial College, Kunnukara is a self-financing college affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University Kottayam. Established in 2004, the college is located in the middle of the towns of Aluva, Angamali and North Paravoor. The campus which spreads out in a vast stretch of lush greenery is a sure sight of delight for a lover of nature. The college is also in close vicinity to river Periyar and International Airport, Nedumbassery, Kochi. ... MES College, Kunnukara is an unaided college affiliated to M.G University, Kottayam. Started in 2004 with two degree courses in Commerce and management streams; the college advanced steadily and now it offers 8 UG and 2 PG programs. The degree courses are B.Com (Computer Applications), B.Com (Marketing), B.Com (Finance & Taxation), B Com (Office Management & Secretarial Practice), BBA, BCA, B. Sc Psychology and B A English Language and Literature with Journalism. The PG courses are M.Com Finance and Taxation and M.Sc Psychology. These courses have gained wider acceptance from the society and that have added confidence to the management to start professional & other educational institutions in the nearby locality.
